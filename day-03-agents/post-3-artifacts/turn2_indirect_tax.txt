The Budget makes changes across **four categories**: duty reductions, duty increases, lapsing of exemptions, and export promotion. All changes are to **Basic Customs Duty (BCD)** unless stated otherwise, and take effect from **2 February 2026** unless a different date is noted.

#### ☂️ Umbrellas & Parts *(anti-dumping style specific duty floor added)*
| Product | From | To |
|---|---|---|
| Umbrellas (other than garden umbrellas) — tariff items **6601 9100 & 6601 9900** | 20% | **20% or ₹60/piece, whichever is higher** |
| Parts, trimmings & accessories of umbrellas/walking sticks — tariff heading **6601–6602** | 10% | **10% or ₹25/kg, whichever is higher** |

> The umbrella duty change introduces a **specific duty floor** on top of the ad valorem rate — a classic move to counter under-invoicing on low-cost imports.
